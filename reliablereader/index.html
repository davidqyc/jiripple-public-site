iOS Reading App

ReliableReader

ReliableReader helps users import long-form text, generate speech audio, and listen through a focused playback workflow on iPhone.

Built for Long Reading

ReliableReader focuses on long-form listening instead of short clips. It is designed for users who want to review text while walking, commuting, or working away from the screen.

User-Controlled Setup

Speech generation uses a user-configured service endpoint. The app is designed to keep the core reading workflow clear and understandable.

Privacy Information

The privacy policy explains what text may be processed, how optional feedback works, and how to contact the developer for privacy questions.
